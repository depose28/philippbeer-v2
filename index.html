The Google years
I joined Google in Dublin in 2011 on the enterprise side, working on acquisition and go-to-market for Google's business products across EMEA. Later moved to London and shifted to hardware — leading go-to-market strategy for Pixel, Google Home, and Chromecast across 15+ markets. The Nuri story
Joined as the first C-level hire to build out marketing, growth, BI, and communications — eventually leading a team of 40+ people. Helped scale the platform from 10K to 300K users and grew assets under management from near-zero to over €500M. Led the rebrand from Bitwala to Nuri and handled fundraising communications through Seed to Series B. It was a wild ride at a crypto-meets-banking startup in Berlin, and I learned a lot about what it takes to scale something fast in a regulated market.
